How to prevent viruses



  1. Check computer has updated anti-virus software
  2. Do not download executables or documents from the internet
  3. Do not open files that end with ".vbs" (sign of a virus program)
  4. Discard email messages that have subject lines of "I Love You"
  5. Discard any other subject line that may look suspicious
