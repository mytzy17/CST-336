ILOVEYOU VIRUS



ILOVYOU (Love Bug) Virus is known as a computer virsus or worm.

It all began in the year of 2000, when an email that said "I love you"

spread among the email's of others. Basically, this email had an attachment

that when clicked it would open. It would then send an email to users that are

recipients of Micorsoft Outlook address book. This virus expanded so quickly

that it reached about 45 million users in a day. So as a result this virus

had to be immediatly shut down before other email users got the deadly virus.
