Welcome to Earth!




The Earth is a rocky and terrestrail planet that has a solid,
active surface. It has mountains, valleys, canyons, and so
much more. Another thing is that 70% of planet is covered by
water. The atmosphere is mostly nitrogen and has a lot of
oxygen. Additionally, a day on Earth is 24 hours and a year
is 365 days. The Earth is the only planet that has a moon. Venus
and Mars are Earth's neighbor planets.
