Welcome to Mercury!



Mercury is the smallest in the solar system. It is the closest
planet to the sun and one of the rocky planets. The surface is
solid and it is covered with craters. Also it has an atmosphere
that is thin, and has no moons. It takes up about 59 Earthdays
to do a full rotation. In addition, a year on Mercury occurs very
quickly due to the fact that it is close to the sun. This means
that it does not take that long to go all the way around. So, it
completes one revolution around the sun in just 88 Earthdays.
