Welcome to Venus!



Venus is the planet that is the hottest even though
it is not close to the sun. It has an atmosphere that
is thick and it is full of greenhouse gases, carbon
dioxide, and clouds of sulfuric acid. The gases gets
heat and it keeps the planet from getting super warm.
This planet also contains volcanoes and mountains. It
spins on the opposite direction of the Earth and it
rotation is very slow, meaning it takes up about 243
Earthdays to spin once. Venus also has no moons
like Mercury.
